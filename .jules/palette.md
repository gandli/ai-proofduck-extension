## 2024-05-18 - Missing Focus States and Tooltips on Custom UI Controls
**Learning:** When building custom interactive elements (like the toggle switches or icon-only buttons in the settings panel), default browser focus outlines are often removed intentionally or accidentally by utility classes like `focus:outline-none`. This breaks keyboard accessibility. Furthermore, purely visual indicators for switches lack native tooltips for mouse users.
**Action:** Always ensure the trio of `title` (for native mouse tooltips), `aria-label` (for screen readers), and explicit keyboard focus indicators like `focus-visible:ring-2 focus-visible:ring-offset-2` (when default outlines are removed) are present on custom switches and icon-only buttons to support comprehensive accessibility.
